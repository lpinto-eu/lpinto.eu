Background

Since I join the Computers and Telematics Engeneering course at Aveiro University, I felt in love with programming and software buildng.

For my Master's Degree thesis, I managed to work in a industry like enviroment. I joined a Health IS team developing a network connecting Hospitals - RTS. My work included the creation of a set of SOAP Webservices, security configurations and semantic services for translation and transcoding.

My Master Degree Theses proved to be a job well done, and because of that I was hired to do it all over again. iUZ Technologies had a Health Business Management Sistem that needed to be exposed throw a Webservices API.

Nowadays

Currently I am working for PT Inovação as an outsourcing developer.

I am a server side developer, currently working with: Java, REST, Json, MongoDB. A dedicated Software engineer, highly committed to develop robust and maintainable solutions. Solid knowhow based on a strong academical background enhanced by a professional experience fulfilled with acknowledged good work - exciting challenges and lessons learned. Most of all, I'm always engaged to learn more on how to create even better software.

Triying to follow with the programing trend, I realised that frontend development and frontend technologies, are becoming a huge deal. In order to expand my technical knowladge, I started this summer learning some frontend specific languages and framworks.
